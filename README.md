# appaday-064-zen-garden
A karesansui you rake with your finger. Carve grooves in raked sand with a finger, three-tine, or six-tine rake, drop a smoothing brush over what you want gone, and tap ripples that wrap around whatever they meet. Set stones, mossy stones, pebbles, maple leaves, blossoms, and a stone lantern, then drag them and watch the sand furrow behind.
